Visit Burj Khalifa 2023

At first glance, when you crane your neck upwards to gather the magnanimity of Burj Khalifa's height, there's one word that comes to mind: overwhelming.

You feel minute, completely intimidated by the tonnes of concrete, steel, glass and aluminum used to create this 21st Century-defining architectural masterpiece. Named after UAE's President, Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Burj Khalifa was inaugurated in 2010 as the tallest building in the world, towering at 820 meters. To put this into perspective, to reach Burj Khalifa's top, it would take almost 5000 6ft-tall humans perched on top of the other.

Travelers from all over the world visit Dubai to head to the top of this man-made wonder. A Burj Khalifa ticket allows you to head to At the Top and At the Top Sky floors, which have the highest observation decks in the world. The view from these Burj Khalifa observation decks is simply breathtaking: Dubai’s surreal skyline full of high-rise towers, the Arabian Gulf and an awe-inspiring sunset are in store for you.

Which Burj Khalifa Tickets Should You Choose?

1. If you're looking to save time2

The Burj Khalifa is one of Dubai's most popular attractions due to which you can almost always find long lines at the site. Book Burj Khalifa skip-the-line tickets to save time and bypass the long queues.

2. If you're looking for a great view at a great price

Burj Khalifa At The Top tickets satisfy both these criteria perfectly. These standard tickets are available at flexible prices with discounts and provide a fantastic view of the city from Levels 124 & 125.

3. If you're looking for a luxurious experience

Visit At The Top SKY! At Level 148, you're about 555 m above the ground - a truly awe-inspiring experience. Moreover, the lounge at Level 148 will treat you to the finest Arabian hospitality, complete with delicious refreshments.

4. If you're looking to cover more in Dubai - and save time

Dubai's roster of experiences is never-ending. If you want to cover the essentials in a short time, book Burj Khalifa combo tickets. Along with entry to Burj Khalifa, get to visit one or more top attractions of the city like Dubai Aquarium, Dubai Frame, Ice Rink, WildWadi Water Park, IMG Of Adventure, Laguna Waterpark, and more.

Burj Khalifa Floors

The primary Burj Khalifa floors open to the public for tourism are At The Top, At The Top SKY and The Lounge.

At the Top

Marvel at Dubai's stunning landscape from Burj Khalifa At The Top floors. The observation decks are located on the 125th and 124 floors of the Burj Khalifa. Purchase a Burj Khalifa At The Top ticket and take the world's fastest double-decker elevators to get to these levels, from where you can use high-powered telescopes to observe the length and breadth of the City of Gold. They are decorated in traditional mashrabiya style, giving you an authentic Arabian feel.

Explore Burj Khalifa At The Top

At the Top SKY

Level 148, known as Burj Khalifa At The Top SKY, is located 555 meters above sea level; it’s safe to say your view from here would be unparalleled and unforgettable. Admire the bird's eye view of the city and its important landmarks from the world's highest observation deck. With an At The Top SKY ticket, you get a personalized guided tour of the area and some refreshments, after which you can step onto the observation deck and admire the jaw-dropping view of Dubai's skyline.

Explore Burj Khalifa At The Top SKY

The Lounge

Unwind, groove and indulge in a delicious meal at The Lounge, located on Levels 152, 153 and 154 of the Burj Khalifa. At 585 meters above sea level, visitors can have meals like breakfast and high tea, which includes a variety of continental dishes and beverages. You also have the opportunity to watch a fabulous sunset or dance the night away at The Lounge, perfectly complemented by a glass of bubbly and some gourmet snacks.

Burj Khalifa Restaurants

What better way to end a long day exploring Dubai than by indulging in a delicious meal with your friends and family. Here are the top 3 we love, but you can choose from several Burj Khalifa restaurants, each that highlight elegant interiors and continental dishes that burst with flavors.

Atmosphere

Situated 442 meters above sea level, Atmosphere at the Burj Khalifa’s 123 level, is dubbed as the world’s highest restaurant. Visitors are in for a treat! Treat yourself to the finest European cuisine amidst sophisticated, warm interiors. What’s more is that you get to dine overlooking Dubai’s impressive skyline. You can book a table that overlooks the dancing Dubai Fountain, sure to be a spectacular show.

Must-try dishes: Steak, salmon, oysters

Timings: 07:00 AM to 12:00 AM

Armani Ristorante

Sink your teeth into some fantastic Italian dishes at Armani Ristorante. The chefs beautifully recreate traditional Italian dishes like spaghetti and tiramisu all the while adding a contemporary touch. Famed Armani hospitality is strongly identifiable with the classy yet stylish interiors. The secluded environment makes it a perfect setting for a small gathering of friends and family.

Must-try dishes: Burrata, lobster spaghetti, risotto Tartufo

Timings: 07:00 PM to 11:30 PM

Armani Amal

Extravagant interiors and a spicy medley of flavors are in store at the Armani Amal restaurant at the Burj Khalifa. It specializes in creating fresh takes on traditional Indian dishes. The food is supremely flavorful and a true toast to South Asia cuisine. Situated at the 3rd floor of the Burj Khalifa, Armani Amal serenades guests with classical musicians playing instruments like the tabla.

Must-try dishes: murg makhni, zatooni jhinga and chocolate cardamom dacquoise.

Timings: 07:00 PM to 11:30 PM

If you're looking for a way to enhance the experience of dining at Burj Khalifa, book a table on New Year's Eve at restaurants like At.mosphere. The view of the Burj Khalifa fireworks from here is simply spectacular!

Beyond Burj Khalifa

Here are the 3 most-visited attractions around the Burj Khalifa in Downtown Dubai:

Dubai Mall

Known worldwide as the largest mall in the world, a visit to the Dubai Mall should be at the top of your itinerary. It's difficult to comprehend just how vast the mall is; it is home to one of the largest aquariums in the world, a wide variety of restaurants, cafes and bars and an ice skating rink, along with almost every designer label imaginable. Since you need to walk through the Dubai Mall to get to the Burj Khalifa, you can indulge in all your fantasies before you exit.

Dubai Aquarium

Befriend King Croc, some sand tiger sharks, stingrays and hundreds of different fish species at the Dubai Aquarium. Sure to be the experience of a lifetime, the Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo is home to several beautiful and diverse creatures. While you can view the exterior for free, be sure to opt for a tour that allows you to walk through a cylindrical tunnel through the aquarium, giving you a complete view of all the exotic species within. You can also try scuba-diving and feeding the sharks!

Dubai Fountain

The reputation of the dancing fountain in Dubai precedes it; prepare to be mesmerized by a spectacular sound, movement and light show put forward by the Dubai Fountain. Located outside the Dubai Mall, right at the foot of the Burj Khalifa, a show is put on by the fountains every 30 minutes after 06:00 PM. Brightly illuminated jets of water are sprayed high into the air, moving to the rhythm of catchy music like Michael Jackson's Thriller, sure to leave you, and your family and friends, enthralled.

Is it worth visiting Burj Khalifa?

Absolutely! The world's tallest building has earned the right to call itself an unmissable attraction in Dubai. Topping off at a staggering 830 m in height, no other skyscraper in the world offers a view as the one from Burj Khalifa's observation decks. If you think At The Top (Levels 124 & 125) offer a breathtaking view, wait till you get to At The Top SKY (Level 148), where you are about 555 m above ground! If that doesn't convince you, the Burj Khalifa is also home to some of the world's best restaurants, boasts fantastic interiors, and offers guests a one-of-a-kind lounge experience.
